What is TfCD?

TfCD stands for "Time for CD" and is a popular arrangement between models and photographers on the internet. The basic idea is that no money changes hands, but both sides benefit from the arrangement. Both, the model and the photographer, will sign a TfCD contract before the shooting starts. The model will receive a CD/DVD containing the photographs after the shooting. These photographs can then be used for prints, displaying on a website or use for her/his sedcard. The photographer in turn can display the photographs on his website. Model and photographer may not use the photographs for any commercial use.
